## New Pitches (pick your favorite or roast them all):

### 1. **"Domino Collapse"**
Each letter is built from **standing dominoes**. When the sketch starts, they're all upright, perfectly forming "GENUARY". Then ONE domino tips... and the entire word **cascades down in a chain reaction**, letter by letter. But here's the twist: as they fall, they **reveal a second message underneath** - or they fall INTO new positions that spell something else entirely.

**Why it works:** Dominoes are instantly recognizable. The collapse is satisfying. The surprise is the transformation.

---

Two knobs at the bottom. A single continuous line draws "GENUARY" like an **Etch A Sketch** - you see the line traveling, making sharp turns, never lifting. The line is **metallic silver on red** (that iconic Etch A Sketch look). Maybe it occasionally "shakes" and partially erases, then redraws.

**Why it works:** Everyone knows Etch A Sketch. One line = no font. The drawing process IS the art.
