## ‚ú® MY ARTISTIC VISION: "Resolution of Memory"

**The piece:**
- A canvas of large, chunky pixels (maybe 16x16 or 20x20 grid)

**The feeling:** Nostalgia, the unreliability of memory, the beauty found in limitation, the ghosts in the machine.

**Color palette:** Deep teals, warm ambers, soft lavenders, and occasional hot pink glitches ‚Äî like a sunset remembered through a Game Boy screen.
